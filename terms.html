  1. Is the Game of Skill Legal?
  • Since games of skill are not included by Indian gambling laws, such as the Public Gambling Act of 1867, they are lawful.In the decisions of State of Andhra Pradesh v. K Satyanarayana (AIR 1968 SC 825) and KR Lakshmanan v. State of Tamil Nadu (AIR 1996 SC 1153), the Indian Supreme Court ruled that a game is considered a game of skill if the player's superior knowledge, skill, attention, experience, and skill are the primary factors that determine their success.
  • The previously contest(s) mentioned higher than (across the Sportiza11 Services) are skill-based games, with the main factors determining a participant's success being their superior knowledge of cricket, football, basketball, hockey, handball, volleyball, kabaddi, American football, and/or baseball statistics; players' relative form; players' performance in a specific territory; conditions and/or format (e.g., ODIs, test cricket, and Twenty20 in the cricket fantasy video game); the contestant's attention and dedication towards the contest(s); and their skillful performance in the contest(s). Participants in the Contest(s) must also form evenly matched teams with constrained resources and make timely replacements in order to score as many points as possible.
  • By participating in this Contest(s), each Participant understands and accepts that he/she is engaging in a game of skill.
