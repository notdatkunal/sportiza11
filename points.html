Rules about substitutions: 

  • In accordance with our Fantasy Point System, each X-Factor substitute will receive four points for being announced and additional points for any contributions they make. 
  • Concussion and COVID-19 Substitutes will receive four points for participating (only in T20s, ODs, Tests, T10s, and The Hundred), and they will receive points for all of their contributions according to the Fantasy Point System, if allowed under the relevant tournament rules and regulations.
  • No points will be given for any contributions made by substitutes other than those for concussions, COVID-19, or X-factor.
  • In the Big Bash League, a player will receive credit for his efforts if he is substituted out for an X-Factor player and later returns to the pitch. A player will not receive credit for any of his efforts, though, if he enters the game as a replacement and was not included in the lineups that were declared (except from COVID-19 and concussion substitutes)
