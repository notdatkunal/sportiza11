Points System

There are minor variations in the Fantasy points across cricket formats. In T20I, for instance, six points are earned if a bowler's economy rate is less than five runs per over. But in Test cricket, there are no points at all depending on the economy rate. In a Test match, four points are taken away if a batter is removed for a duck. Nevertheless, two points are subtracted in a T10 or T20 system if the identical scenario occurs.

Just to let you know, the scoring system differs depending on the format. This is a thorough explanation of the points system in fantasy cricket. All forms share one thing, though, and that is the captain and vice-captain receive two times and one-half the points, respectively.
