Responsible Play

Sportiza11: What is it? 


 One of the best fantasy sports games to play online cricket leagues is called Sportiza11. Users may create their own squad based on their sports expertise and perhaps earn money. 
 
How can I register? 


 Users can register on Sportiza11 by completing a brief registration form found on the website's homepage. On the other hand, users may also register straight through Google+ or Facebook.

 

Why am I unable to access my account? 


 Users may experience difficulties with registration for a number of reasons. The user may be inputting their email address or password incorrectly, which is one of the main reasons they are unable to join up. Below the login option, there is a Forgot Password link in case the user has forgotten their password. The user will be sent to a new webpage after selecting the "Forgot Password" link. There, they may reset their password by providing their registered cellphone number, and Sportiza11 will give them an OTP. To reset their password, the user can input the OTP that was supplied to them by Sportiza11.

 

Can I use Sportiza11 to create several accounts?

 
 No. On Sportiza11, a person may only have one account under their name with legitimate supporting documentation. For further information, please refer to the Fair Play policy. 
 
Can I make changes to my information? 


 By utilizing their registered email address and password to access their Sportiza11 accounts, individuals may effortlessly amend or update their personal data. Users can amend or update their information by clicking on "Edit Profile" in the upper right corner of the page after login into their Sportiza11 account. Once registered with Sportiza11, individuals will be able to alter their basic information, such as their date of birth, profile name, and address, but they will not be able to change their email address, cellphone number, or team name.

 

I joined up, but I never received a confirmation email. 
 
 There might be two reasons why you might not have gotten a confirmation email from Sportiza11 following your registration: 
 
 The email sent by Sportiza11 may have been categorized as "Spam" and directed to your mailbox's spam folder. In order to prevent any more annoyance, in such a situation, kindly check your mailbox's spam folder and label Sportiza11 as "Not Spam." You should also add Sportiza11 to your contacts. 
 
 It's possible that the user entered their email address incorrectly while joining with Sportiza11. Please use our Contact Us page to get in touch with us in such a situation.

 

I have little knowledge of cricket; can I still participate? 


 Sportiza11's online fantasy cricket game services are accessible to all players, including those with less cricket knowledge. Therefore, even if you don't know much about cricket, you may still play. Additionally, by providing you with information on playing strategies, the "How to play" portion of the Sportiza11 website may help you with the game.
