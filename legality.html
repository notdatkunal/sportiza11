Legality

 

The legitimacy of the game of skill


 In most jurisdictions, fantasy sports competitions are regarded as games of skill. However, state laws and national laws may differ regarding the legality of fantasy sports competitions. It is your duty to confirm that it is allowed in your area to participate in fantasy sports competitions. 

 


Qualifications

 

For you to be qualified to enter a competition or be awarded a prize on the Sportiza11 website, you have to fulfill the following requirements:

 

  • You have to be at least eighteen years old. 
  • To access the Sportiza11, you must be a legitimate resident of the nation in which you are doing so. 
  • You must not live in a place where it is illegal for you to participate in fantasy sports competitions.
  • It is required that you do not work for Sportiza11 or any of its subsidiaries.
  • You cannot be a direct relative of a worker of Sportiza11 or any of its subsidiaries.
